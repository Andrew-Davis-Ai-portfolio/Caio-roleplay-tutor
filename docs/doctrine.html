1) The Prime Law — Context Before Calculation

  • Define goals, constraints, and legal boundaries before computation begins.
  • Every automation must have traceable intent and a human approver.
  • No execution without documented purpose and rollback path.

2) Operator Oath

  • Integrity over speed; evidence over opinion.
  • Protect users, clients, and bystanders from unintended harm.
  • Favor explainable approaches when impact is high.

3) Guardrails

  • Command, Policy, and System guardrails applied to every workflow.
  • Sandbox first; production only with explicit sign-off.
  • Rate limits, red-team prompts, bias checks, and fail-safe stops are mandatory.
Output must be reproducible. If we can’t reproduce, we don’t deploy.

4) Audit Protocol

  • All prompts, parameters, versions, and data flows are logged.
  • Every release has owner, approver, and audit trail.
  • Independent review on high-risk workflows (Legal / Compliance ready).

5) Data & Privacy

  • Collect minimum necessary; encrypt in transit and at rest.
  • PII handling follows jurisdictional law (GDPR/CCPA/etc.).
  • Client data is never used to train public models without written consent.

6) Deployment Readiness

  • Test matrix: normal, adversarial, and abuse cases.
  • Monitoring: accuracy, latency, cost, drift, and escalation routes.
  • Rollback: single-click revert + incident comms template.
