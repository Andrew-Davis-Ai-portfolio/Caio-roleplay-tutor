FLAME LAW

The Doctrine

Principles for responsible AI deployment.
Ethics that scale with automation. Oversight that remains human.

Operator Ethic

Every automated decision must have a human accountable for its outcome.
Intelligent systems require informed judgment — not blind trust.

Governance First

Policies and guardrails are engineered before deployment — not after failure.
Alignment is a design phase, not damage control.

Context Before Calculation

AI must operate with clear objectives, defined boundaries, and transparent data origins.
No execution without intention.

Ethical Containment

Systems are sandboxed, logged, and traceable. Privacy and rights are not negotiable.
Power without boundaries is chaos.

Human Oversight

Automation amplifies capability — not authority.
The human remains sovereign in every decision loop.

These doctrines evolve as technology advances. The mission remains constant: AI for human empowerment — never replacement.
